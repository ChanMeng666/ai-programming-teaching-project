## Three steps to repeat at home

1. **Open claude.ai** and type a prompt.
2. **Play your toy** with the keyboard.
3. **Clip on a banana** and make it yours.

All three prompts are on the [student handout](/docs/2026-peyvand-academy/resources/handout).

## Safety first

<Warning>
Before connecting Makey Makey hardware, read these rules carefully.
</Warning>

- **Only use food, foil, and playdough** as conductive objects.
- **Hold the metal Earth bar** so the circuit works.
- **Never connect anything to mains electricity.**
