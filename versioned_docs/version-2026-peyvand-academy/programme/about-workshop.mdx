**Youth Tech Series — AI & Electronics Workshop**

*AI & Electronics Workshop for Youth (Ages 12–18)*

An interactive workshop for young participants aged 12–18, exploring Artificial Intelligence concepts and basic electronics through hands-on learning — delivered by SheSharp in collaboration with Peyvand Academy and the Ministry of Education.

## About the Session

Join us for an interactive session exploring the world of Artificial Intelligence and Electronics.

SheSharp, in collaboration with Peyvand Academy and the Ministry of Education, hosted this workshop for young participants aged 12–18. The session aimed to build foundational knowledge in emerging technologies through hands-on learning.

Participants explored key AI concepts while gaining practical exposure to basic electronics in a supportive and collaborative environment.

## What to Expect

- Introduction to Artificial Intelligence concepts
- Basic electronics exploration
- Interactive activities and discussions
- A collaborative learning environment
