## SheSharp

**SheSharp** is a not-for-profit organisation founded in 2014, dedicated to supporting women in technology and digital careers across Aotearoa New Zealand.

SheSharp exists to inspire, educate, and connect women in tech. The organisation delivers programmes, workshops, and community events that support people at every stage of their career journey — from those taking their first steps into tech to experienced professionals looking to grow.
