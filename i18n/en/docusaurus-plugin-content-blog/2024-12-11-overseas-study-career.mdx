## ðŸŽ“ About Further Education

When applying for overseas graduate programs, you need to pay attention to the following key points:

### The Value of Work Experience
- Domestic development work experience can provide some advantage
- However, the impact is relatively limited
- Focus on highlighting practice experience relevant to your field

## ðŸ’¼ Overseas Employment Considerations

### Key Factors in Major Selection

1. **Job Market Demand Orientation**
   - Research talent demand in your target country
   - Understand popular industries and positions
   - Analyze future market trends

2. **Immigration Policy Considerations**
   - Whether you intend to immigrate locally
   - Understand local immigration policies
   - Assess the relevance between your major and immigration

3. **Personal Career Planning**
   - Clarify long-term career goals
   - Evaluate professional development prospects
   - Consider personal interests and abilities

## ðŸŒŸ Suggestions and Reminders

### Pre-Application Preparation
1. Thoroughly research your target country
2. Understand the job market situation
3. Study changes in immigration policies
4. Assess your personal competitiveness

### Planning Key Points
- Set clear goals
- Create a detailed timeline
- Prepare sufficient funds
- Get language preparation done in advance

:::tip Recommendation
Before making a decision, it's recommended to:
- Talk with people working/studying overseas
- Attend relevant lectures and sharing sessions
- Follow policy changes in your target country
- Develop multiple backup plans
:::
