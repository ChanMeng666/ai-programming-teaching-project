## Community

TechNest Community is a Newfoundland-based nonprofit making technology accessible, creative, and impactful. Beyond the AI mentorship cohort we run:

- **Tech in the Park** outdoor networking events.
- **Sip & Paint** creative-meets-tech evenings.
- **Library Labs** AI-powered 3D learning platform development.
- **Tech for Children** programmes in partnership with NCANL.

See [About TechNest](/docs/programme/about-technest) for the full picture.
